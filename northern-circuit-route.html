Northern Circuit Route – Mount Kilimanjaro

The Northern Circuit Route is the longest route on Mount Kilimanjaro and offers excellent acclimatization and very high summit success rates.

It circles around the northern slopes of the mountain and provides incredible panoramic views with fewer crowds.

Route Highlights

  • 9 day Kilimanjaro climbing route
  • Very high summit success rate
  • Beautiful panoramic scenery
  • Professional mountain guides
