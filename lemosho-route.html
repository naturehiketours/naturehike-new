Lemosho Route – Mount Kilimanjaro

The Lemosho Route is considered one of the most scenic routes on Mount Kilimanjaro. It approaches the mountain from the west and offers spectacular views of the Shira Plateau.

This route is perfect for climbers who want a longer trek and better acclimatization, which increases the chances of reaching Uhuru Peak.

Route Highlights

  • 8 day Kilimanjaro climbing route
  • Beautiful panoramic views
  • High summit success rate
  • Professional mountain guides
