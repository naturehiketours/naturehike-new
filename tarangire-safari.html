Tarangire National Park Safari

Tarangire National Park is famous for its large elephant herds and ancient baobab trees. It is one of Tanzania's most beautiful wildlife parks.

During the safari you can see elephants, lions, giraffes, zebras, wildebeest, and many other animals in their natural environment.

Tour Highlights

  • Large elephant herds
  • Beautiful baobab landscapes
  • Game drives with professional guide
  • Amazing wildlife photography opportunities
