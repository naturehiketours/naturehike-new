Serengeti National Park Safari

Serengeti National Park is one of the most famous wildlife destinations in the world. It is home to the Great Migration where millions of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles move across the plains.

During your safari you can see lions, elephants, cheetahs, giraffes, and many other animals in their natural habitat.

Tour Highlights

  • Game drives across Serengeti plains
  • Great Migration viewing
  • Professional safari guide
  • Comfortable safari vehicles
