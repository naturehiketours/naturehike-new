Ngorongoro Crater Safari

Ngorongoro Crater is one of Africa’s most beautiful wildlife destinations. The crater is home to lions, elephants, rhinos, buffalo, zebras, and many other animals.

It is often called Africa's natural wildlife paradise because animals live inside the crater throughout the year.

Tour Highlights

  • Game drive inside Ngorongoro Crater
  • See the Big Five animals
  • Professional safari guide
  • Comfortable safari vehicles
