Marangu Route – Mount Kilimanjaro

The Marangu Route is known as the "Coca-Cola Route" and is one of the most popular ways to climb Mount Kilimanjaro.

It is the only route with comfortable mountain huts instead of camping, making it a popular choice for many climbers.

Route Highlights

  • 6 day Kilimanjaro climbing route
  • Comfortable mountain huts
  • Beautiful forest and alpine landscapes
  • Professional mountain guides
