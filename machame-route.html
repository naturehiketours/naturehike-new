Machame Route – Mount Kilimanjaro

The Machame Route is one of the most popular routes for climbing Mount Kilimanjaro. It is known for its beautiful scenery and excellent acclimatization profile.

The route takes climbers through rainforest, moorland, alpine desert, and finally to the summit at Uhuru Peak, the highest point in Africa.

Route Highlights

  • 7 day Kilimanjaro climbing route
  • Beautiful landscapes and views
  • High summit success rate
  • Professional mountain guides
