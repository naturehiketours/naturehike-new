Rongai Route – Mount Kilimanjaro

The Rongai Route approaches Mount Kilimanjaro from the northern side near the Kenyan border. It is one of the quieter routes and offers a unique perspective of the mountain.

This route is ideal for climbers who prefer fewer crowds and a gradual ascent.

Route Highlights

  • 7 day Kilimanjaro climbing route
  • Less crowded path
  • Beautiful views of the Kenyan plains
  • Professional mountain guides
